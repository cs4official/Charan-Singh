Exhibitions & Showcases

Students register and create their projects directly on the Student Corner platform. Teams and individuals use a collaborative editor to build out a project page with text, images, documents, video, audio, and so much more! Registration

Projects & Galleries

Unlock creative thinking and teamwork in an instant. Showcasing student work on Student Corner is simple: students create projects that can be grouped and featured in galleries right on your organization's homepage.

Feedback & Collaboration

Student Corner seamlessly connects students so that they can work together on projects and ideas. Students, teachers, evaluators, and admin can use the on-platform feedback system to leave valuable insights right on a project page.
